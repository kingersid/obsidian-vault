## Phase 1–4 Protocol

### Phase 1: Gut Rest (Days 1–3)
Warm water, coconut water, moong dal water, jeera water, rice kanji. Zero digestive load.

### Phase 2: Gentle Reintroduction (Days 4–7)
Khichdi, curd rice, buttermilk, steamed idli/dosa (mild), upma with lauki.

### Phase 3: Building Flora (Days 8–14)
Add fiber gently: poha, pulao, steamed veggies, fermented rice cakes, moderate spices.

### Phase 4: Maintenance (Days 15+)
Daily curd + buttermilk, variety of fermented foods, regular meal times, warm ginger water.

## Daily Protocol (Vegetarian)

| Time | Protocol |
|------|----------|
| Morning | Warm water + ginger or saunf (1 tsp soaked overnight) |
| Breakfast | Poha / upma / idli / curd rice |
| Lunch | Rice + moong dal + lauki sabzi + jeera OR khichdi |
| Evening | Buttermilk (chaas) with roasted jeera + mint OR banana |
| Dinner | Khichdi / curd rice / 2 idlis (light, before 8 PM) |
| Bed | Warm water OR warm milk + pinch turmeric |

**Daily non-negotiables**: curd or buttermilk, warm water, regular meal times.

## Key Foods

- Prebiotics: banana (slightly green), oats, flaxseed, jeera, saunf
- Probiotics: curd, buttermilk, idli/dosa batter (fermented)
- Soothing: moong dal, lauki, khichdi, stewed apple, papaya
- Avoid: raw salads, heavy dal (toor/chana/rajma), fried foods, spicy street food, excessive dairy, cold water with meals

---

## Red Flags

- Diarrhea returning or worsening after Day 3
- Blood in stool
- Fever above 100°F returning after Day 2
- Severe abdominal pain or bloating
- Persistent vomiting
- Signs of dehydration (dark urine, dizziness)
