## Key Risks
1. **Overvaluation** — priced for perfection
2. **LGD structural threat** — commoditization → pressure on certification fees
3. **Blackstone overhang** — 76.55% stake, likely exit 2026–2027
4. **Geographic concentration** — ~80% India revenue
5. **Working capital deterioration** — debtor days: 33 → 80
6. **Declining returns** — ROE: 83% → 58% post-IPO
7. **AI disruption** risk to grading
