### 2. Key Risk Factors
1. **Valuation Risk**: Paying for perfection in an imperfect industry
2. **LGD Structural Threat**: Commoditization affecting certification fees
3. **Blackstone Overhang**: PE firm holds 76.55%, likely exit in 2026-2027
4. **Geographic Concentration**: ~80% revenue from India operations
5. **Working Capital Deterioration**: Debtor days increased from 33 to 80 days
6. **Declining Return Ratios**: ROE from 83% to 58% post-IPO
7. **Competition**: Wider/narrower than advertised, AI disruption threat

### 3. Financial Metrics Trend
- **EPS Decline**: Sep-25: ₹3.22 → Dec-25: ₹3.04 (5.6% QoQ decline)
- **OPM%**: 78% (Mar-25) → 70% (Dec-25) - margin compression
- **Revenue Growth**: 22% YoY but receivables growing 3.6x faster
- **Working Capital Days**: 25 (Mar-19) → 173 (Dec-25) - concerning expansion

### 4. Price Targets
- **Current Market Price**: ₹362 (P/E: 28.5x | Mkt Cap: ₹15,636 Cr)
- **Bear Case Target (12M)**: ₹240 (▼ ~34% downside)
- **Deep Bear/Stress Case**: ₹180 (▼ ~50% downside)
- **Fair Value Band**: ₹220–260 (▼ 28–40% downside)

### 5. Bull Scenarios (Threats to Bear Thesis)
- **LGD volumes grow 30%+ p.a. for 5 years, ARP holds**: Low-Medium (20%)
- **Blackstone delays exit beyond 2028**: Medium (35%)
- **IGIL expands into non-gem certification**: Low (10%)
- **Global ND market recovers strongly**: Medium (30%)

### 6. Final Verdict
"Excellent Business. Wrong Price. Wrong Time."

**Conclusion**: The market has priced perfection. Reality rarely delivers perfection. Wait for ₹220–260 range to offer a meaningful margin of safety. At current price, risk-reward is firmly against the buyer.
