

## Chandni Silk Mills LLP

It's been a solid month at the mill. We dispatched 3,200 metres of Chanderi silk and 1,800 metres of pure georgette to buyers in Surat, Mumbai, and Jaipur. The Jaipur order alone — 900 metres of block-print georgette for a boutique there — came through a reference, which is always the best kind of business.

Production is running at about 85% capacity. We had a 4-day loom downtime last week because two of the older power looms needed servicing. That's sorted now. The new jacquard attachment is giving us cleaner borders on the Banarasi range — early feedback from the trade has been good.

Receivables are at ₹14.2 lakhs outstanding. About ₹9 lakhs of that is within 30 days, so nothing alarming. The ₹5.2 lakhs older than 60 days — two buyers, both long-standing — follow-up is due this week.

One challenge: raw material costs, especially pure silk yarn, have gone up about 8% since March. We haven't passed it on yet. That conversation with buyers needs to happen before the next season order cycle in July.

Overall, steady. Not spectacular this month, but the foundation is solid.

Monthly store revenue is tracking at ₹68,000 against a target of ₹75,000. Achievable if the next two weekends hold up.

## Label Ethnic Vogue — Online Store (labelethnicvogue.shop)

Website had 1,240 sessions this month, up from 890 last month. The Instagram traffic is finally converting — about 18% of sessions came from the bio link. Still thin on direct traffic, which means brand recall is still building.

We got 14 online orders this month. Average order value is ₹3,100. 9 of those were new customers, 5 were repeat. The repeat rate is the number I'm watching most carefully.

The Kalamkari kurta set at ₹2,450 is the top-selling SKU — 4 units sold. The Mul cotton readymade at ₹1,850 is a close second.

Cart abandonment is still high — around 61%. Two reasons: shipping cost showing up late in checkout, and no COD option yet. Both are solvable. COD through Shiprocket should be live in the next few days.

One win: a customer from Bengaluru left a 5-star Google review this week. She mentioned the packaging. Small thing, big signal.

Monthly online revenue: ₹43,400. Last month was ₹31,200. The trajectory is right.
